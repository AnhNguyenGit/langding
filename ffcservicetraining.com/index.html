Financial Franchise

What is franchising?

Franchise is an agreement in which the franchisor (one party) grants or licenses some rights and rights to the franchisee (another party). Franchising is a well-known marketing strategy for business expansion. A contractual agreement takes place between the franchisor and the franchisee.

Financial Franchise

In traditional business, we often hear about the Commercial Franchising of big brands like Mac Donalds, KFC, Pizza Hut ...
In the field of Finance, the concept of Franchising is also applied in developed countries such as the US, UK, France, Canada, Australia ...
For example:
You are a Franchise Broker (FB), you introduce a trader or an investor to participate in the financial market (commodities, stock, foreign exchange (forex) etc.) by registering a direct link provided by yourself.
